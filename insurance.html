Most Asked Questions

1. How old do I have to be to buy an Insurance?

The minimum age to purchase insurance varies by type and location, but typically, individuals can buy certain types of insurance, like auto insurance, at 18. For other types, like life insurance, you might need to be older. We recommend checking specific policy requirements or contacting our agents for guidance.

2. What is KidCare Insurance?

KidCare Insurance is our specialized insurance plan designed for children. It covers a range of needs from healthcare to education and accident coverage, ensuring comprehensive protection for your child's growing years.

3. Do you accept credit cards?

Yes, we accept various forms of payment, including credit cards. Our goal is to make the payment process as convenient as possible for our clients, offering multiple options for your ease.

4. How do Insurance plans work?

Insurance plans work by transferring the financial risk of life's uncertainties from an individual to an insurance company. You pay a premium, and in return, the insurance company promises to cover specific financial losses during the term of the policy.

5. Who is eligible for the Future Insurance plan?

The Future Insurance plan is designed for individuals who are looking for long-term financial security and coverage. Eligibility may vary based on age, health, and other factors. We recommend consulting with our agents to determine your eligibility.

6. Why should I have Insurance?

Having insurance is essential as it provides financial protection against unexpected events such as accidents, illnesses, or property damage. It ensures peace of mind by safeguarding your financial future and that of your loved ones.
